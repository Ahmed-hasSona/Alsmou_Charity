Welcomes you

The idea

The organization originated and developed at the hands of philanthropists to fight poverty and theft and support low-income families in 2013. The idea of ​​the organization is to help segments of low-income families to live their lives like other people

Targets

Supporting the economic, social and psychological loss, combating harmful habits in all its forms, caring for needy families, sponsoring orphans, and working to upgrade health services

future plans

The organization will focus its efforts by covering the needs of widows under the slogan (I and the sponsor of an orphan in heaven are like these two). We also have a charitable clinic, a suitable study environment for orphans, and digging wells for drinking water.

The organization was born

The work and preparation for the establishment and restructuring of the system and foundations of the Al-Smou Charity Organization began since September 2013, as meetings continued during that period between the Alumni Club and the Al-Kut Charitable Organization. The main purpose of its establishment is to fight poverty and the hardship of living that has decimated many Sudanese
